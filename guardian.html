The Guardian Project

The Guardian Project is a secure, lightweight contact-tracing and alert system for COVID-19 exposure which can integrate with verified test results and symptom self-reporting. Our mission is to preserve a high level of privacy and be easily usable by anyone, regardless of age or technical affinity.

I was one of the top contributors among an international team of developers working on the open-source client. I implemented fully interactive flows based on wireframes supplied by a team of designers and wrote the functionality for many of the components to generate content and intake data. I collaborated with teammates to pass the collected information to the Redux-store for processing by the rest of the application.
