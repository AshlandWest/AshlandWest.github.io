HiFi Registration

High Fidelity Fusion is an international festival celebrating an emerging style of partner dance.

We are building an advanced online registration system that will completely automate the process of accepting donations to our affordable-pricing (AP) fund and allocate the money from that fund to applicants, based on their financial needs.
